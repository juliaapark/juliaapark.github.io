Text___HitoSteyerl
Hito Steyerl is a German filmmaker,
visual artist, and writer whose work
explores the power of images in a
digital world. She investigates how
technology, media and circulation affect
knowledge, culture and society. In her
essay In Defense of the Poor Image,
Steyerl reflects on the life of low-
resolution, compressed, and frequently
copied images, arguing that these “poor
images” become a form of resistance,
creativity, and solidarity in a media
landscape driven by perfection and control.
